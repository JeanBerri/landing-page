Welcome to the Great Pirate Era!

One Piece follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and his crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, exploring the seas in search of the mythical treasure known as the "One Piece" in order to become the next King of the Pirates.

The Four Emperors

Captain Buggy the Genius Jester, leader of the Cross Guild boasting powerful pirates such as Mihawk and Crocodile and Chairman of Buggy's Delivery. Former Warlord of the Sea.
"Straw Hat" Monkey D. Luffy, captain of the Straw Hat Pirates hailing from the East Blue. Ate the Gum-Gum fruit as a kid and now stretches. Possible alias: Nika the Sun God (aka) "Joy Boy".
Red-Hair Shanks, captain of the Red Hair Pirates and the longest reigning of the current emperors. Gave Luffy the infamous straw hat in windmill village in the East Blue. Powers relatively still unknown, but he is powerful.
Marshall D. Teach (aka) Blackbeard, captain of the Blackbeard Pirates. Only person in known history to be able to wield two devil fruit powers. Murdered a former crew member when serving the Whitebeard Pirates.

Wealth, fame, power. Gold Roger, the King of the Pirates, attained everything this world has to offer. And so, many men head for the Grand Line to find the great treasure he left behind: the One Piece. The world has truly entered a Great Pirate Era! Wearing the straw hat sworn upon him by the great pirate, Shanks, Monkey D. Luffy heads out to the sea on a journey to become King of the Pirates!

-Eiichiro Oda, author of One Piece
